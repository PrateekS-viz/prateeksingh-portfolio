About me
A decade of BI - pipeline to boardroom

I'm an Analytics & BI Leader with 10+ years building data infrastructure that organisations run on - ETL pipelines, Medallion architecture, governance frameworks, and C-suite dashboards across HR, finance, and operations.

My edge is the full stack without the handoff: I can architect a Bronze-to-Gold pipeline in Databricks, design the star schema, build the Power BI semantic model, and walk a VP through the insights - all in the same week. Done this at Jackson National Life, Red Hat, and Infosys across Fortune 500 environments.

Before I was an engineer, I was an educator. Five years at Infosys designing BI curricula and delivering 400+ sessions to professionals across three continents gave me a lens most data leaders don't have - I know how to make complex data genuinely understandable. That instinct shapes how I build dashboards, run governance reviews, and coach analysts.

Currently a Dean's Fellow in the MBA at Kelley School of Business (Indiana University, #1-ranked online MBA) - focused on Strategy & Product Leadership, because the best analytics leaders understand the business, not just the data.
